About the App

AniBase

A local-first anime library server for watching your own collection from a PC, phone, tablet, or TV on the same LAN, with background metadata enrichment, watch progress, automation, and a polished streaming-style interface.

What It Does

Built for local anime libraries

Point it at your anime folders and start browsing as soon as the local scan finishes. Posters and AniList information continue loading in the background.

01

Browse Your Library

Home, anime detail, movies, seasons, studio pages, schedule, filters, search, and sorting.

02

Continue Where You Left Off

Watch history, progress badges, resume buttons, and episode status help you keep momentum.

03

Play Your Way

Watch in the browser, open files in Media Player, or hand playback to MX Player on supported Android devices.

Core Experience

A streaming-style UI for your folders

Library View

  • Featured titles and Last Watched at the top.
  • Release and watch-status filters for larger collections.
  • Readable poster badges for Not Started, Watching, Up To Date, and Completed.

Player Flow

  • Soft episode switching without a full page refresh.
  • Next and Previous keep the viewport on the player while centering the active episode in the queue.
  • Subtitles, screenshots, progress reporting, toast feedback, and Discord Rich Presence.

Management

Small tools that make local libraries easier

Auto Import

Watch a downloads folder, move matched files into the right anime folder, and manually resolve unmatched files.

Quick First-Run Setup

Choose library folders and a theme, open the library after the local scan, then let metadata finish in the background.

Settings Clarity

Status cards show Anime Library, Movie Path, Player, Discord, Auto Import, and LAN readiness at a glance.

Local / LAN Note

Designed for a trusted home network

AniBase is intentionally local-first. It is made for your own machine and devices on the same LAN, not for public internet hosting. Streaming pages remain easy to access from nearby devices, while sensitive local actions use lightweight protection.

  • SQLite library index and JSON settings/cache files.
  • AniList enrichment, resilient episode-number parsing, Media Player launch, and media helpers.

Project Personality

A personal streaming shelf, not a public platform
